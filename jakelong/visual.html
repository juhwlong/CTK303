VISUAL.

Reading Is Harder

This is a piece to show how it looks to read with my specific learning disability. This disability is not on the books so doctors aren't sure how to treat it. This is the first installment of a series about experiencing how others learn with thier learning disabilities.

Inner Self

This work was created for an assignment where I had to make art that was uncomfortable to me in a way. The way this was uncomfortable for me was that I have little to no experience with making visual art. The concept of the piece is that no matter what people say about you and say you should be, you need to keep true to your inner self.
